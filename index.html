About Us

Hi, I’m Adam Thepaut, founder of Thepaut Risk Advisory. I specialize in political risk analysis—helping clients navigate election outcomes, cross-border tensions, and macroeconomic shifts with clarity and confidence.

While I may be early in my career, I'm driven, precise, and relentless in pursuit of insight. In just a short time, I've already delivered accurate predictions—including forecasting a Trump landslide as early as August.

I hold a degree in International Studies from the Université de Montréal and am currently pursuing a Master’s in International Affairs at HEC Montréal.

My work blends sharp analytical instincts with real-time global awareness, offering a clear framework tailored to your strategic needs.

Services

Each engagement includes a one-on-one session where we:

  • Risk Analysis of Political and Geopolitical factors

    Identify key political drivers, elections, policy shifts, social unrest—and assess cross-border threats, regional conflicts, and strategic chokepoints affecting your operations.

  • Scenarios & Mitigation Strategies

    Outline scenario probabilities and create mitigation strategies for the three most likely outcomes.

  • Economic Impact Forecasting

    Project the economic impact of each scenario and their subsequent mitigation strategies.

  • Strategic Reunion Presence

    Participate in strategic meetings with key stakeholders to evaluate project viability and identify potential liabilities.
